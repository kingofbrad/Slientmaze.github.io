OUR REASONIN!!

We wanted to make a game that would allow people with hearing impairments to be on the same level as other people without the impairment. So, we decided to come up with a VR game that is completely based on vibration and sight.

The game is based on a randomised maze that the player would have to transverse through and collect 4 keys to escape the maze. However, there is an invisible creature following the player and if the creature catches up with the player, they LOSE.

As mentioned before, the player is within VR and there is NO sound whatsoever. There are ways to hide from the creature by going towards lockers and chests to hide.
